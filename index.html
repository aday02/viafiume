Living the dream in Abruzzo, Italy

Welcome to our corner of Italy! We've made Pescara our home away from home, and we're excited to share our experiences, favorite spots, and the beauty of this remarkable region with friends and family.

Our Home on Via Fiume

We found our perfect spot in the heart of Pescara. Here's a glimpse into our daily life and the spaces we've made our own.

Living Space

Our comfortable living area has two couches and a TV with WIF for relaxing and watching Italian TV.

Kitchen

We have a simple kitchen with a table for six along with all the basic necessities for a great time.

Kids Room

This room is ready for all the kids with a bunk bed and a single bed. There is also a fourth mattress under the twin for a fourth kid.

Guest Room

There is a guest room with a queen bed for a couple to relax and enjoy themselves.

Main Bedroom

The main bedroom is at the end of the hall with a queen bed and a desk for those of us that have to work from home.

Bathroom

There are two bathrooms each with a shower and bidet. The guest bathroom also has a washing machine.

Make Yourself at Home

Our home is designed to make you feel comfortable and welcome. Here's what makes it special:

  • WiFi - available throughout the house, SSID: viaFiume
  • Stove - propane stove with 4 burners and oven
  • Dishwasher - under sink
  • Washing Machine - ONLY (no dryer but there is a hanging rack)
  • 65" LCD Screen - with Netflix and Prime Video
  • Individual AC Units - per room controlled by remotes
  • Manual external window shades - Don't lift them too high
  • Piano - for musical entertainment if you want
