Detailed Directions

Final Approach to Via Fiume

  1. Enter Pescara and look for signs to Pescara Centrale Station (Stazione Centrale) or Corso Umberto I
  2. Navigate to Via Fiume - it's very close to the train station
  3. Look for the building with Intesa Sanpaolo bank on the ground floor
  4. The apartment entrance is on the backside of the building (not on the street with the bank)
  5. The apartment is located above the bank at Via Fiume, 15
  6. Parking is available at Pescara Centrale bus station just across the street

Travel Tips

Car Rental

We recommend renting a car for maximum flexibility exploring Abruzzo. Three rental companies are located at Pescara Centrale train station:

  • Europcar - Our favorite option
  • Avis
  • Budget

Book online, then walk over to their office at the train station to get your keys.

Electric Scooters & Bikes

Increase your exploration radius with Bit Mobility electric scooters and bikes. Simply rent them through the app on your phone!

You can travel several miles north or south along the dedicated bike lane on the beach boardwalk, making it easy to explore the Adriatic coast.

Fuel Stops & Autogrills

Gas stations on highways accept credit cards. In small towns, some may be cash-only.

Don't miss the experience of stopping at an Autogrill (Italian truck stop)! They're fun destinations with unique local items, amazing coffee, and delicious snacks. Stop and grab an espresso just for the experience!
